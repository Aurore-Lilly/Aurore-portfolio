Instant Pet Finder:
Bowser & Mobile Prototype

My client, Instant Pet Finder, wants to create a new adoption website. This website will allow users to contact local shelters about adoptable pets, as well as search for pets of their choosing in their area through the website. My role was to design a sign up form for the new site.

Role: UX / UI Designer Course: Team Treehouse Techdegree

It was essential to the team that the form was both mobile and web-friendly. The form uses a standard design pattern. Users can sign up using the email form or their social media account. The ability to use their social media logins speeds up the registration process which can can give the users the motivation to reach their goal, even if they are lacking the time at the moment. And it eliminates the need to remember a password.
